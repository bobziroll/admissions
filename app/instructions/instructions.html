Instructions

Welcome to the V School admissions project! Your task, simply put, is to "hack" this website.

The purpose of this admissions project is to test both your resilience and resourcefulness. A lot of what you'll be required to do may be completely foreign to you and could even prove frustrating to figure out.

We've included clues throughout the texts to help guide you in the process, but you will be required to figure certain things out for yourself. You may use any resource available to you EXCEPT asking for help from another person. As a starting point, you should definitely do some research on the "Chrome Developer Tools," as they will be invaluable to you in this process. In real-life situations, you're highly encouraged to get outside help after spending a good amount of time trying to figure things out on your own, but for the purposes of this project you'll need to do everything by yourself.

For this project, we highly recommend the following setup:

  1. Resize your browser's window to mostly or completely fit the screen you're using.
  2. Dock your Chrome Dev Tools on the bottom instead of on the right of the screen. For instructions on how to do this, check this Stack Overflow post.

Although you will be asked to look at and debug parts of this site, no previous programming experience is necessary to complete this challenge.

You should use the Chrome browser to complete this challenge, so if you're not currently using the Chrome browser, please download it now and visit this site from the Chrome browser instead.
