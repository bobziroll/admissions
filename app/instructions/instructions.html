Instructions

Welcome to the V School admissions project! Your task, simply put, is to "hack" this website.

The purpose of this admissions project is to test both your resilience and resourcefulness. A lot of what you'll be required to do may be completely foreign to you and could even prove frustrating to figure out. You are not required to finish this within a certain amount of time.

We've included clues throughout the texts to help guide you in the process, but you will be required to figure certain things out for yourself. You may use any resource available to you EXCEPT asking for help from another person. As a starting point, you should definitely do some research on the "Chrome Developer Tools," as they will be invaluable to you in this process. In fact, if you work your way through this Code School course from beginning to end and can mostly understand what it teaches, this project should be fairly simple for you.

In real-life situations, you're highly encouraged to get help from other people after spending a good amount of time trying to figure things out on your own, but for the purposes of this project you'll need to do everything by yourself.

For this project, we highly recommend the following setup:

  1. Required: Use the Google Chrome web browser to complete this project. You can download it here.
  2. Resize your browser's window to mostly or completely fit the screen you're using.
  3. Dock your Chrome Dev Tools on the bottom instead of on the right of the screen. For instructions on how to do this, check this Stack Overflow post.

If you close this page, your progress will be reset.

Although you will be asked to look at and debug parts of this site, no previous programming experience is necessary to complete this challenge.
